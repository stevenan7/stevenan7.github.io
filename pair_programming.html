Why Pairing?

Pairing is a collaborative process between DBC'ers where two individuals of varying strengths are able to work together to accomplish a common goal.

How to pair

Usually there will be one person driving and the other will be navigating.
The driver is the person who actually works at the keyboard.
The navigator guides the driver and has the big picture view of the project.

Reflection

I'm still getting used to occupying the roles of driver and navigator--it's hard not to want to type something in when navigating rather than explaining it, and it's equally difficult to stay focused on what the navigator is saying when driving--but with each pairing session I think I'm improving. In my GPS, we alternated roles whenever appropriate on the fly, and for challenge 5 since I was having trouble dealing with screensharing and updating the HTML page and using devtools at the same time, I mostly navigated. I still have a lot to improve, but I'm looking forward to getting better with each of my pairs.
