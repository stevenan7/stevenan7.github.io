Asking a good question is like giving good feedback: it should be kind, specific, and actionable. You can show kindness by being mindful of the askee's time. Phrase the question like you are asking a busy colleague. Be specific about what information you are seeking so that the askee knows if they can help you or not. Asking an "actionable" question means that it is clear to the askee how they can respond. A bad question leaves the askee wondering what the asker is even getting at, a good question is clear and considerate to the askee. (add more)
