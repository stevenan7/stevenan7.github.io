TESTING

My Gregorc thinking style tended towards the abstract styles--I had even scores on both abstract random and abstract sequential with lower scores in the concrete category. According to Gregorc, people who are abstract learners like theory and people centric open ended work environments, as opposed to concrete thinkers, whose styles are more based in reality. I've found my struggles to be related to my thinking style, as I have initially had trouble driving and have found navigating and seeing the big picture or theory behind the code much easier. A concrete sequential learning style would be ideal for a driver, which was the area I was least represented in, so this week during the GPS I made sure to drive and work on that area.
I've also been struggling with the little details of code--like syntax and organization--perhaps my abstract big picture thinking style needs to be honed down to be more concrete and detail oriented.
